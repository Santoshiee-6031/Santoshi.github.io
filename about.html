ABOUT Newari culture

Newars are the traditional residents of Nepal.

Newars are regarded as the original inhabitants of the Kathmandu Valley.

Newars are known for their contributions to culture, art and literature, trade, agriculture and cuisine. Contact us to purchase beautiful newari dresses and learn more about newari culture.
